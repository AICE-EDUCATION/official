About Us

Know About AICE

Ayush Institute of Computer Education

AICE is a premier computer education institute dedicated to providing high-quality, practical, and industry-oriented training to students. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world technology demands.

With a strong focus on hands-on learning and personalized mentorship, we prepare our students to excel in the ever-evolving field of technology.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with cutting-edge computer skills and foster a culture of innovation and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized institute that produces skilled, ethical, and forward-thinking technology professionals.

Why Choose AICE?

  • Industry-relevant curriculum
  • Experienced and supportive faculty
  • Hands-on practical training
  • Affordable fee structure
  • Certificate of completion
  • Career guidance and support
  • Modern infrastructure
  • Flexible batch timings
